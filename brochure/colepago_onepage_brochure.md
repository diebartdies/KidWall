## How It Works

### 1. Parent Funds Wallet

![Parent Funding](https://dummyimage.com/100x80/6c757d/ffffff&text=Parent+Funds)

- Parent uses the app to add money via Stripe/Mercado Pago.
- **No card data or real money ever touches our backend.**

### 2. Virtual Tokens Credited

![Tokens](https://dummyimage.com/80x80/36b37e/ffffff&text=Tokens)

- Backend receives payment confirmation and credits virtual tokens/coins to the child’s wallet.
- Only tokens/coins flow through the backend.

### 3. Child Spends at School

![Child Pays](https://dummyimage.com/100x80/f6c23e/ffffff&text=Child+Pays)

- Child uses tokens to pay at school canteen, store, or for activities.
- Parent receives instant notification.

### 4. Security & Protection

![Security](https://dummyimage.com/80x80/1cc88a/ffffff&text=Secure)

- **AI Face Detection & Biometrics:** Only the right child can access the wallet.
- **No Real Money Exposure:** All real money handled by PCI-compliant providers.
- **Data Privacy:** No sensitive payment data stored in the app or backend.
